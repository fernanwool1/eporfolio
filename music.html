Music as Memory

Music

Every melody carries a version of me.

From the quiet corners of my past to the wide horizons of tomorrow, music has been the way I remember, express, and become.

Sound
Identity
Roots
Emotion

My Instruments

Tools of expression. Extensions of my soul.

Guitar

My first love. Six strings that hold a thousand stories.

Ukulele

A smaller sound with the same big heart.

Piano

Where thoughts become melodies without words.

Violin

Strings that ache and sing in the same breath.

Zampoña

The voice of the Andes. Heritage in every breath.

Kalimba

Small notes, big magic. Simple sounds, infinite resonance.

Pipa

A pear-shaped sound from a world away from home.

Lyre Harp

An ancient shape for a still-unfolding story.

The Feelings Behind the Sound

Nostalgia

Melodies that take me back to the moments I never want to forget.

Healing

Music that mends what words cannot and brings me back to myself.

Heartbreak

The songs I wrote when silence was the only thing that understood.

New Beginnings

Rhythms that remind me that every ending is also a doorway.
